CRM Implementation / Custom Development

Our team is composed of consultants and developers who are experts in CRM platforms like Salesforce and Microsoft Dynamics.

Customer Relationship Management (CRM) implementation and custom development are two critical aspects of building and maintaining successful customer relationships. CRM systems help businesses manage interactions with customers, while custom development enables businesses to tailor the CRM to their unique needs and workflows.

CRM implementation involves setting up and configuring a CRM system to manage customer interactions effectively. This includes identifying the data that needs to be captured and creating workflows to manage customer interactions. A well-designed CRM implementation can help businesses improve their customer experience, increase sales, and improve customer retention.

Custom development involves creating solutions within the CRM system to meet the specific needs of a business. This could involve creating custom fields, reports, or workflows that are tailored to the unique requirements of the business. Custom development can help businesses streamline their processes, reduce manual work, and improve efficiency.

Product engineering is an essential part of the product development lifecycle, and it plays a crucial role in the success of a product. It helps businesses to identify opportunities for innovation, create new revenue streams, and improve the customer experience. By leveraging the latest tools and technologies, product engineering teams can bring products to market faster, with higher quality and at a lower cost.

Product engineering encompasses various activities, such as market research, ideation, design, prototyping, testing, validation, and launch. It requires a deep understanding of the user's needs, the market trends, and the technological capabilities.

Development

We provide custom Salesforce development services using Aura, LWC, Visualforce, Apex etc. to help our clients build custom applications, components, and integrations.

Customization

We help our clients customize their Salesforce systems to meet their specific needs, including customizing fields, workflows, reports, and dashboards and visualforce pages.

Integration

Salesforce integration: We help our clients integrate their Salesforce systems with other business systems,including ERP, marketing automation, and other third-party applications, for maximum efficiency.

Migration

We help our clients migrate their data from other CRM systems to Salesforce, ensuring data integrity and minimal downtime.
