Our Engagement

Solution We Provide

No matter what your needs are, we have a solution that suits You

Managed Services

In this model, Allshore takes full responsibility for managing the client's IT staffing needs. This includes sourcing, recruiting, onboarding, training, and managing the IT professionals. This model provides a turnkey solution for clients that need to manage their IT staffing needs but do not have the resource or expertise to do so.

Project-based/Staff Augmentation

In this model, clients engage Allshore professionals to work on a specific project or set of projects. This model provides flexibility and allows clients to manage their costs effectively.

Contract Staffing

In this model, clients engage Allshore professionals on a contractual basis for a specific period, which can range from a few months to several years. This model is ideal for companies that need IT professionals for specific projects or short-term assignments.

Contract-to-Hire

In this model, clients engage Allshore to provide resources on a contract basis with the intention of hiring them as full-time employees at the end of the contract period. This model allows clients to evaluate the IT professionals' skills and fit with the company culture before making a long-term commitment.

Direct-Placement

In this model, Allshore recruits IT professionals for clients to fill permanent positions. Allshore charges a one-time fee for the service. This model is ideal for clients that need to hire IT professionals for permanent positions but do not have the resources or expertise to recruit them.
